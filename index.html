    Who is Unthethered Magic?

    Syowia Kyambi

    is the main concept and curatorial creator of Untethered Magic. She builds networks and facilitates exchange in support of research and process based practices. Syowia is particularity interested in knowledge building through the merger of different disciplines. Often bringing together different age groups, areas of study as well as merging artists with creatives in the field of humanities and sciences.

    What We Offer

    Untethered Magic is an organic, self-sustaining abode that provides an array of facilities. The space has numerous shared open-air spaces, which foster workshops, residencies, exhibitions, documentation, and other collaborative projects.

    The space can cater to up to 40 people, as it’s fully equipped with catering facilities and a contemporary kitchen. Among other facilities include digital amenities, where the residents and visitors can broadcast, take photos, record music, shoot vlogs & cinematic videos that can be edited and processed on Untethered Magic’s workstation. We also provide an art studio and library that make it possible to research, conceptualize and produce contemporary or personal work.


    Untethered Magic

    Artist Led Residency Space in Nairobi, Kenya. Para-institutional programme for artistic and cultural development.
